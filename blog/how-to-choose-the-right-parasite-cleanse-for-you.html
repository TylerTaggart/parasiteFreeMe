What Are Parasites, and Why Should You Care?

Parasites are tiny organisms that live in or on your body and take nutrients from you to survive. Some common parasites in humans include:

  • Protozoa: These are single-celled organisms that can multiply inside your body, like giardia.
  • Helminths: These are larger worms, such as tapeworms or roundworms.

How Do Parasites Affect Your Health?

Parasites can cause a variety of symptoms depending on the type and severity of the infection:

  • Digestive problems: Gas, bloating, constipation, or diarrhea.
  • Fatigue and brain fog: Feeling constantly tired or struggling to focus.
  • Skin issues: Rashes, itching, or unexplained acne.
  • Weight changes: Unintentional weight loss or gain.

Many people live with mild symptoms for years without realizing they have a parasitic infection. That's why doing a regular parasite detox cleanse can help you stay ahead of the game.
