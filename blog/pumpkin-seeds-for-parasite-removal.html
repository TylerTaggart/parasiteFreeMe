How Pumpkin Seeds Work to Eliminate Parasites

Pumpkin seeds contain an amino acid called cucurbitacin, which has been shown to paralyze parasites, particularly intestinal worms. This paralysis prevents the parasites from holding on to the walls of the intestines, making it easier for the body to expel them naturally through bowel movements. This makes pumpkin seeds an effective and gentle option for those looking to cleanse their body of parasites without resorting to harsh chemicals.

The high fiber content of pumpkin seeds also aids in the expulsion process. Fiber helps to bulk up stool and move it through the digestive tract more efficiently, which in turn helps to flush out the paralyzed parasites. Additionally, the anti-inflammatory properties of pumpkin seeds can help soothe the digestive tract and reduce the inflammation often caused by parasitic infections.
